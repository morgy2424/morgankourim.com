Feel Better. Live Better.

Sympta provides complete allergy testing and treatment service in already existing healthcare facilities. The goal of this campaign was to bring awareness to patients that their healthcare facility offers allergy testing as well as educate patients that their allergies are common and treatable. Photography and impactful headlines were effectively utilized to reach a wide audience. The campaign was executed across print and social media platforms.
